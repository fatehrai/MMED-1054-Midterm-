Mountains or Beaches?

Arguements for why Mountains make for a better vacation destination.

Mountainous places clearly make for a better vacation experience because of the following reasons:

  • They allow you to get far away from society so you can have alone time with yourself of the few close loved ones you are travelling with.
  • The views are far superior than beaches...I mean who wants to look at plain old sand and water all day?
  • There is a lot more exploring that can be done in mountainous areas with all the trails you have access to.

Mountain vacations always make for a happy camper!

Arguements for why Beaches make for a better vacation destination.

Mountainous places clearly make for a better vacation experience because of the following reasons:

  • The weather that can be enjoyed on a tropical beach beats the cold weather in the mountains.
  • You can cool down with a swim in water that is the perfect temperature.
  • Beaches are much safer because there is no risk of falling off a cliff.

A day at the beach takes all your worries away!s
